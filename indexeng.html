Hemp de Mallorca About us

We are a company of local farmers who are dedicated to the field for several generations, with experience in crops, vegetables and aromatics, always implementing the organic processes throughout the production chain. We currently project ourselves at the birth of industrial and medicinal hemp. We have all the documentation and permits required by the European Union. Our crops have one of the most favorable climates for their development, the Mediterranean, the lands of Mallorca and the currents of marine winds, for which our product is unique, they acquire excellent conditions with some nutrients that only occur in Mallorca, in the southern area, which strengthens and provides nutrients to plants with these properties unique.

Our Mision

Our mission apart from protecting the earth is to develop the industry of industrial and medicinal hemp in mallorca for the world, providing organic products with the only quality standards that can only be produced in Mallorca, outdoors, naturally.
